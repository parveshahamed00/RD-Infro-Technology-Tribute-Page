About Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. He is known for his remarkable leadership in expanding Tata Group's business ventures and making it a global name. Under his leadership, Tata Motors introduced the world's cheapest car, Tata Nano, and acquired prestigious international brands like Jaguar Land Rover. Beyond his business achievements, Ratan Tata is admired for his humility, philanthropy, and commitment to social causes, contributing significantly to India's education, health, and development sectors.
